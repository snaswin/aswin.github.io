About me

Aswin Raghav Nirmaleswaran

Cornell University

Meinig School of Biomedical Engineering

Aswin is motivated by the fact that Computer Vision can be used to make healthcare more personalized. He wants to build smart devices which can integrate Healthcare into every component of people’s life.!

Currently a masters student at the Meinig School of Biomedical Engineering, Aswin is exploring various facets of Medical technology. He is focusing on Computer Vision, feature extraction and instrumentation to augment his experience and knowledge. He is inspired by companies that build futuristic products that make people’s life better through innovation.

Prior to graduate school, Aswin strengthened his foundation in Biosignal Acquition and processing, Product Design and Instrumentation!
